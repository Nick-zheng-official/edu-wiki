## Long-term Project Notes (100-Chapter Plan)

### Phase 1: Daily Life (Chapters 1-20)
- **Focus**: World-building, character development, daily training
- **Foreshadowing density**: Low (1-2 per chapter)
- **Conflict intensity**: Low
- **Goals**: Establish relationships, deepen training, introduce minor mysteries

### Phase 2: Build-up (Chapters 21-50)
- **Focus**: Reveal more backstory, develop conflicts, activate dormant foreshadowing
- **Foreshadowing density**: Medium (3-4 per chapter)
- **Conflict intensity**: Medium
- **Goals**: Uncover villain motives, explore Fenrir's story, expand world

### Phase 3: Rising Action (Chapters 51-80)
- **Focus**: Major conflicts, character growth, significant foreshadowing payoff
- **Foreshadowing density**: High (5-6 per chapter)
- **Conflict intensity**: High
- **Goals**: Escalate threats, reveal major secrets, prepare for final battle

### Phase 4: Conclusion (Chapters 81-100)
- **Focus**: Final battles, resolution of all arcs, epilogue
- **Foreshadowing density**: Very high (all remaining resolved)
- **Conflict intensity**: Peak
- **Goals**: Final showdown, complete character arcs, satisfying ending

### Key Guidelines
- **"Knife" moments**: Use sparingly, only at emotionally significant points
- **Character growth**: Ensure steady progression for all main characters
- **World consistency**: Maintain established rules and lore
- **Pacing**: Allow breathing room between intense chapters
